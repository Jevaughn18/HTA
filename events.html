Upcoming Events

22nd Annual Holy Convocation

Dates: 7–11 Mar 2025

Location: Harvest Temple Portsmouth, St. Catherine

Join us for HTA's Annual Convocation — a powerful time of refreshing, restoration, and revival. Featuring dynamic day speakers, anointed nightly preachers, and impactful messages rooted in the Apostolic doctrine, this gathering is designed to strengthen the body of Christ and ignite your spiritual fire. Expect powerful worship, fellowship, and a special emphasis on prayer and fasting.

Schedule Highlights:

  • Day 1 (7 Mar): Opening Ceremony & Worship Service
  • Day 3 (9 Mar): Youth Empowerment Session
  • Day 5 (11 Mar): Closing Revival & Celebration

All are welcome — come expecting a mighty move of God! Registration is encouraged; contact (876) 123-4567 for more info.

HTA Youth Camp 2025

Dates: 10–16 Aug 2025

Location: Knox Community College Cobbla, Manchester

Get ready for HTA Youth Camp — a life-changing experience filled with fun, fellowship, and faith-building sessions. This camp features inspiring devotionals, interactive workshops on leadership and faith, powerful worship services, and outdoor activities like sports and team-building exercises. It’s designed to empower young people to grow deeper in Christ and stand boldly in truth.

Activities Include:

  • Day 1 (10 Aug): Welcome & Icebreaker Games
  • Day 3 (12 Aug): Leadership Workshop with Guest Speaker
  • Day 6 (15 Aug): Talent Night & Praise Celebration

Contact the Youth Department at youth@harvesttemple.org for registration details and fees. Limited spots available—register by 15 Jul 2025!
