About Us

The National Youth Department at Harvest Temple Apostolic is dedicated to empowering and inspiring the next generation of believers. We focus on fostering spiritual growth, leadership development, and community engagement through dynamic programs, worship, and outreach initiatives.

We encourage active participation in events, mentorship, and service projects, ensuring our youth are prepared to fulfill the Great Commission.
