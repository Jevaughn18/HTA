Frequently Asked Questions

We emphasize modesty in all things. Ladies are asked to wear dresses or skirts, and we encourage everyone to dress respectfully as we come before the Lord. While many wear their Sunday best, the most important thing is that you come with a modest appearance and a heart ready to worship God.

Our Sunday service typically lasts about 3 hours, including Sunday School, worship, and the preaching of God's Word. However, we yield to the leading of the Holy Spiritâ€”if He moves, we're willing to stay longer to worship, pray, and allow God to have His way among us.

We practice water baptism by full immersion in the name of Jesus Christ for the remission of sins, according to Acts 2:38. If you've repented and want to follow Jesus in baptism, we'd love to talk with you about taking this important step of obedience.

We believe in the Oneness of God, water baptism in Jesus' name, and the baptism of the Holy Spirit with the evidence of speaking in tongues. We follow the Apostles' doctrine as outlined in Acts 2:38-42, and we believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God that guides our faith and practice.
